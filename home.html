About MIYIO

A platform to share research findings and development of science and engineering students.
This program has produced many talented students in the world and world-class science
and engineering research projects.


Watch 2019 MIYIO Award Ceremony video below.

Purposes

Creating

Creating a dynamic platform within the scope of STEM by doing research work.

Improving

Improving the communication skills of students in the latest research or innovation.

Cultivating

Cultivating the spirit of STEM among students around the worlds by changing experiences.

Developing

Developing the abilities and creation of students in STEM to a higher platform.

Categories

Innovative Science

Biology, Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Computer Science

Environmental Science

Ecology, sustainable materials and industries health science, climate change, marine science, earth history

Social Science

Psychology, Sociology, Geography, History, Economy, Arts, Education

2. What's the criteria for research?

Introduction

Pages containing background issue, problem, details.

Mobile Friendly

Methods related to the issue.


STEM

Extended Abstract must be in English (Arial 11, Space 1.5, Maximum 5 pages, Margin 3, 3, 4, 3 TBLR) 

Discussion

Discuss issues highlight in the paper based on references or bibliography from article, proven with the scientific result

Conclusion

Contains the conclusion of the discussion that has been describe previously.


Reference

List of reference used in the paper (APA Style)



3. What's the judging criteria?

1

4

5

Questions & Answers

Able to answer live Q&A Session (online meet)
